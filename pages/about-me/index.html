I’m Roberto Mozara — physicist, writer, and developer. My interests combine science, philosophy, programming, and Integral Yoga. I work across theoretical physics, power system modeling, and digital design, with an emphasis on consistency, depth, and clarity.

I completed my PhD in condensed matter physics at the University of Hamburg. Since then, I have continued to study questions of structure and coherence in both scientific and human contexts. This website collects essays, research, and related projects that reflect that focus.

My current work includes energy systems analysis, optimization modeling, and the design of digital environments for complex ideas. I try to approach each field with the same principles of precision and integration.

In recent years, I have also spent time in India, especially at the Sri Aurobindo Ashram in Pondicherry, following a long-standing study of the writings on Integral Yoga.
