Local Lorentz Transformations and Curvature

This seminar paper (in German) was prepared for a proseminar at the University of Hamburg in 2008. It describes local Lorentz transformations and curvature within the fibre bundle approach in differential geometry.

The connection coefficients define the covariant derivative on a smooth manifold and appear as coefficients of the local connection form. Cartan’s structural equations rewrite curvature and torsion tensors in terms of this connection form, defining Lorentz transformations. Physical quantities derived from curvature and torsion remain invariant under these transformations.

The formalism extends naturally to the frame bundle, giving a unified geometric interpretation for tensors, covariant derivatives, torsion, and curvature. The principal fibre bundle framework underlies both general relativity and gauge field theories, providing a common mathematical foundation.
